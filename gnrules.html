Grizzley Nation Rules

Follow these rules to ensure a safe and fun community for everyone.

Community Rules

1. Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

2. No Spam

Do not spam channels or private messages with unnecessary content.

3. No Cheating / Exploits

Cheating, exploiting, or any malicious behavior is strictly prohibited.

4. Appropriate Content

NSFW content or illegal material is not allowed in any channel.

5. Follow Staff Instructions

Always follow directions from moderators and admins. Their decisions are final.

6. No Advertising

Advertising other servers, products, or services without permission is prohibited.

7. Stay in Channels

Use the appropriate channels for your content. Off-topic discussions should be in designated channels.
